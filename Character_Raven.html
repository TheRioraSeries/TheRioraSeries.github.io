Raelyn Faulkner

Other Names: Rae; Raelyn Murray Family:
Father - Asher (Deceased)
Mother - Catriona (Deceased)
Older Brother - Dakim
Age: 17
Race: Dragon
Ability: Dragon/Pure Miutho
Birthday: December 25th
Description:
Barely over five feet tall and a hundred pounds; Has dark brown hair with blue highlights and matching bright blue eyes; Not overly sociable or outgoing, but is capable of making friends; Has a tribal dragon tattoo between her shoulder blades; Isn't quick to judge and tries to see the good in everybody, which makes it hard for her to fight and kill, but she does it to protect her friends; Doesn't like to accept charity and wants to accomplish things on her own; She tries to please everybody, often causing her to pass out from exhaustion by the end of the day.
About:
When on Earth, she lived in Florida. She avoided contact with people because they could often feel the power that flowed through her, although they didn't know what it was. After learning that her brother was accussed of being a mass murderer and coming to Kusnik, she starts training to defeat Raven.
