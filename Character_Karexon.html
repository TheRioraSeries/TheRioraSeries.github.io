Karexon Radev

Other Names: Family:
Unknown
Age: 28
Race: Riora - Vriknir
Ability: Shadow
Birthday: October 12th
Description:
He is almost always wearing a "goofy grin" and is good at lightening up a tense atmosphere with a joke or too. At the same time, he loves to make smart comments to get under people's skin. His relatively bright personality is a stark contrast to his dark ability. He has deep gray eyes and midnight blue hair, although he died his hair brown when he went to Earth.
About:
Not much is known about Karexon before he became a member of Myron's team. He seems to love cooking, since he always cooks even if the team gets together at Myron's house. He loves joking with Raelyn because she is easy to pick on, but he is serious when he is on Earth and sits down to listen to Raelyn express her thoughts and feelings.
