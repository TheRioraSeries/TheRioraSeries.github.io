Myron Masters

Other Names: Family:
Wife - Rose (Deceased)
Son - Blaine (Deceased)
Age: 36
Race: Riora - Vriknir
Ability: Sound Manipulation
Birthday: July 25th
Description:
About:
He was an ambitious young adult until he met a girl named Rose who he fell in love with. He eventually mellowed out as he began to settle down, marrying Rose and having a son named Blaine.

He lost Rose and Blaine in the attack on Vriknir, but found himself questioning the conclusion the investigators reached. Because of this, Myron has no one to blame or be angry at, so instead he finds himself living in the past, constantly staring at the memorial with their names on it.

After learning about Dakim and the Naeku, he finally gets some closure and is able to start moving on. He decides to take Raelyn in, and she becomes like a daughter to him.
