Meet Celeste Hall

Celeste Hall attended St. Vincent-St. Mary high school and graduated 9th in her class. She is currently pursuing a bachelor of science in Computer Science and a minor in Japanese at Kent State University. She works part time on campus as a Security Analyst and helps out at her boyfriend's family restaurant, Emidio and Sons, whenever they need her. She also recently finished up a computer related internship at Cardinal Health.

Celeste first started writing stories when she was 10 years old. She would spend her summers at her grandparent's house and type away on the often unused computer in the back room. Since then, writing has become a big hobby and stress reliever. She participated in power of the pen competitions in middle school and was part of her high school's creative writing club.

Along with writing, Celeste's interests include languages, bowling, line dancing, drawing, anime, and video games. She was also a practitioner of the martial art Soo Bahk Do Moo Duk Kwan for six years and participated in several tournaments.
