Raelyn Adelinda
Other Names:
Rae; Raelyn Murray

Age:
17

Race:
Dragon

Ability:
Dragon/Pure Miutho

Birthday:
December 25th

Family:
Father: Asher (Deceased)
Mother: Catriona (Deceased)
Older Brother: Dakim

Description:

Barely over five feet tall and a hundred pounds; Has dark brown hair with blue highlights and matching bright blue eyes; Not overly sociable or outgoing, but is capable of making friends; Has a tribal dragon tattoo between her shoulder blades; isn't quick to judge and tries to see the good in everybody; Doesn't like to accept charity and wants to accomplish things on her own.

About:
When on Earth, she lived in Florida. She avoided contact with people because they could often feel the power that flowed through her, although they didn't know what it was. After learning that her brother was accussed of being a mass murderer and coming to Kusnik, she starts training to defeat Raven.
