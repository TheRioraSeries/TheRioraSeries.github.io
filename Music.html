Music that inspired Adelinda

See You Again - Carrie Underwood

When Raelyn is in the car with Myron after saying goodbye to Dustin and Christian, I can imagine this song on the radio while Raelyn is reminiscing about all the fun times she had with them.

View Lyrics

Play it Again - Luke Bryan

This fits Dakim’s encounter with Ursula (The daughter of the Naeku leader) in the bar before Vriknir was attacked.

View Lyrics

Easier to Run - Linkin Park

This fits Dakim’s reaction to when his parents are killed and how he goes on the run. It also sort of describes Dakim’s personality in general.

View Lyrics

Falling Slowly - Josh Groban

I have a lot of "favorite songs", so to say that this is one of my favorite songs doesn't mean a whole lot, and I can’t say that it’s my absolute favorite, but I do really like this song.

When Royce asks Raelyn to dance at the banquet (where Royce is being honored for his heroic action and Raelyn is being welcomed to the village), I see this being the song they dance to.

A majority of the song doesn’t really apply to the books. However, the chorus is what inspired me to have Roy change the lyrics written on his wall when Raelyn breaks up with him.
