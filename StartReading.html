A note before you start...

I've reread this story eight or nine times, and I know what it's supposed to say. That makes it very hard for me to make edits properly. I think I've fixed the glaringly obvious ones, but I would really appreciate it if you could make note of you corrections you think need to be made.

Along with grammatical and spelling corrections, I would appreciate it if you would take notes in general about the thoughts running through your head, questions in particular. If you ever find yourself asking, "what ever happened to this?" please make note of that. I have a handful of sub-plots, and I know how they're supposed to resolve, but I need to make sure they actually get resolved in the story.

I really appreciate you taking the time to read the story an help me out. Please send the notes you take to TheRioraSeries@gmail.com. I hope you enjoy reading it as much as I enjoyed writing it.

--Celeste Hall
